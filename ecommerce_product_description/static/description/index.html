eCommerce Product Description

eCommerce Product Description is an excellent solution for describing your products with more freedom. The application adds a separate field under the eCommerce Tab that enables you to present your products with greater details, which will eventually lead to greater sales.

Set Product Description

If you select the "eCommerce" Tab, you’ll find a separate field named "Website Description" as shown in the image below. You can showcase your creativity with words while describing the features or benefits of your product. If you’re seeking a solution to pitch your products, in style, on your online store, eCommerce Product Description is the go-to solution.

Select the "eCommerce" Tab to find the "Website Description" field to describe your product.
